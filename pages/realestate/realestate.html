Real Estate Mariah Gardner, Owen Fitzpatrick, Daniel Kehelly
Watch here.

This film is a comedic take on the quirks of modern real estate and human behavior. It follows William, a smooth-talking real estate agent, as he delivers an over-the-top sales pitch to Jim, a reluctant and somewhat bewildered potential buyer. The situation spirals hilariously when Jim’s fiancée, Diane, arrives, questioning not only the sale but also Jim’s susceptibility to William’s charm. With sharp dialogue and dynamic character interactions, the project explores themes of persuasion, practicality, and the humor in everyday absurdities. It was a joy to contribute to bringing this lighthearted and relatable story to life.
