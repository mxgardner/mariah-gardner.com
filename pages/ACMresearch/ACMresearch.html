Director of Research ACM at UTA

As Director of Research for ACM at the University of Texas at Arlington, I lead initiatives to foster innovation and collaboration within the organization. My role involves coordinating research projects, mentoring members, and facilitating workshops to explore cutting-edge topics in computing. By bridging academia and real-world applications, I aim to empower students to develop solutions that address complex technological challenges, driving both personal and professional growth within the ACM community.
