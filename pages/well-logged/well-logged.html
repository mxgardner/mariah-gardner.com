This project was developed for UT Austin's Jackson School of Geoscience's first open-source hackathon, focusing on bridging the gap between science and accessibility. It provides an intuitive platform for managing and visualizing geological data logs, empowering researchers and students to explore and analyze data collaboratively and efficiently.
